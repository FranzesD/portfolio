About Me

For five years, I led a creative studio, building visual systems, directing multidisciplinary teams, and translating abstract ideas into clear, tangible outcomes.

Over time, my focus shifted from how things look to how they work. I became increasingly drawn to systems — the logic behind interaction, behavior, and structure. That shift led me into interactive design and game systems, where mechanics, flow, and user behavior meet.

Today, I design system-first: structure, escalation, clarity, and intent. Whether it’s a brand, a product interface, or a game mechanic, I turn complexity into something precise and purposeful.
